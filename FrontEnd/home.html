Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

25 mins 24 cookies

Key Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Quick Instructions:

  1. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy
  2. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  3. Mix in flour and chocolate chips
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet
  5. Bake at 375°F for 9-11 minutes

Key Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour

Quick Instructions:

  1. Mix dry ingredients in large bowl
  2. Add wet ingredients and mix well

Easy Cinnamon Rolls From Scratch

2 hours 12 rolls

Key Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar + cinnamon

Quick Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and yeast
  2. Add warm milk and melted butter
  3. Knead dough and let rise
  4. Roll out, add cinnamon sugar
  5. Cut, roll, and bake at 375°F

Homemade Pizza Recipe

Key Ingredients:

Simply Sandwich Bread

3 hours 1 loaf

Key Ingredients:

  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 2 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Quick Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, yeast, water, and honey
  2. Knead dough until smooth
  3. Let rise for 1 hour
  4. Shape into loaf and rise again
  5. Bake at 375°F for 30-35 minutes

Quick and Easy Baked Fish Fillet

20 mins 2 servings

Key Ingredients:

  • 2 fish fillets (cod or tilapia)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Quick Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F
  2. Season fish with salt and pepper
  3. Top with garlic, lemon, and breadcrumbs
  4. Drizzle with olive oil
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes
